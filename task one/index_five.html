about coding Academy

Coding Academy is a dynamic learning hub dedicated to empowering individuals with cutting-edge programming skills. Located in Orange, it offers comprehensive courses on web development, app creation, data science, and more. With expert instructors, hands-on projects, and a supportive community, it’s the perfect place to start or advance your tech career. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, the academy tailors programs to suit every skill level.
“Train and Empower Youth to enhance their opportunities in the digital sector in Jordan”
